About CloudCrack
CloudCrack is a dynamic quiz platform designed to help users test and expand their knowledge across cloud technologies and services. Users can register, select topics of interest, and participate in a variety of quizzes. Each quiz provides instant feedback, correct answers, and detailed results to help users learn and improve.

Key Features:
  • Simple sign-up/sign-in for personalized experience
  • Select quiz topics of your choice
  • Randomized questions and question pools
  • Quiz timer for fair play
  • Leaderboards and scoring
  • View correct answers and explanations
  • Earn rewards and badges for achievements
Whether you're preparing for certifications, brushing up on skills, or just having fun, CloudCrack offers a comprehensive environment for cloud learning and competition.
